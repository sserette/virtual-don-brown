Virtual Don Brown

One of the most valuable assets in the game of football is the ability to anticipate a play call. Virtual Don Brown predicts whether the inputted football play will be a run or a pass. A machine learning model was trained on 500,000 NFL plays ranging from the 2009-2017 seasons. According to Amazon Web Services, the model is 82% accurate, while 18% are errors. Training data can be found on Kaggle.
